## Color Reference

### Backgrounds
- Tab Bar: `rgb(28,28,28)` - Dark charcoal
- Inactive Tab: `rgb(32,32,32)` - Slightly lighter
- Active Tab: `rgb(40,40,40)` - Darker than inactive
- Hover: `rgb(45,45,45)` - Brightest

### Text Colors
- Inactive: `rgb(189,189,189)` - Medium gray
- Active: `rgb(255,213,128)` - Gold/yellow
- Hover: `rgb(224,224,224)` - Light gray

### Accents
- Active Border: `rgb(255,183,77)` - Bright gold
- Close Hover: `rgb(239,83,80)` - Red
- Border: `rgb(48,48,48)` - Dark gray
